Wind Energy

What is wind power?

Wind power is the term for the mechanical energy from wind spinning wind turbines.

How does wind power work?

An inflow of wind to a wind turbine turns the blades and rotor, which then spin the main shaft and gearbox, which then spin the generator, generating electricity.

How much does wind power cost to implement?

Small wind turbines cost around $3k-$5k per kilowatt of power capacity, meaning that for projects desiring 5-15kW of wind power capacity, the estimated price would be around $15k-$75k for a small wind turbine project.

How much money does wind power save in the long run?

One study by Synapse Energy Economics revealed that the incorporation of wind energy in the U.S. Midwest could result in savings for consumers ranging from $65 to $200 annually, meaning that it could save people thousands of dollars within their lifetime.

How do tax benefits regarding wind power work?

A 30% tax credit is applied to the cost of purchasing and installing wind turbines at your main home plus one other.
