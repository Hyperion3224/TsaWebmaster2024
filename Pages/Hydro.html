Dam Hydropower

If you've ever been to a dam, you'll notice that there's a lot of water on one side, and almost none on the other. Dams utilize the want of water to go form being high to being low. As the water moves through a dam, it spins turbines which generate electricity. This electricity is then stored and transported for use.

The Lowest Cost to Rule Them All

In 2020, the median hydropower cost was $45 MW/h, whereas the lowest cost for Fossil Fuels was $55 MW/h (energy.gov, ipcc.ch) Experts predect a widening in the gap between the two as time goes on, leading Hydropower to be the wiser long-term investment.

Tax Rebates- Sweet, Sweet, Savings

To sweeten the deal, the US government offers multiple tac rebates for Hydropower investors. The 2022 IR Act allowed for a minimum return of $5.50 MW/h up to $22.00 MW/h-at the cost of more restrictions (IRA Tax Credit). This is one of the eight total credits available.
